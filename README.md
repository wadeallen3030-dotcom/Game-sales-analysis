# Project Background

Aether Games is a global video game publisher specializing in digital distribution. Following the launch of their hit title "Galactic Frontier," the company implemented a series of aggressive seasonal promotions to maintain player momentum and expand into emerging markets. 

This analysis was commissioned by the Marketing Director to evaluate the commercial impact of the game’s two largest promotional windows on the Windows platform. As the game matured, the marketing department shifted its strategy from "Brand Awareness" to "Revenue Optimization." The Director needed a data-driven post-mortem to determine if the steep discounts offered in the second year actually yielded higher net profitability compared to the first year, or if they simply increased "Returns" and overhead costs.
